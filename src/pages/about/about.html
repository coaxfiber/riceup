Rice Up Application

Introducing the RiceUp App! A simpler way to connect the farmer to the consumer. Just search around you for local products online!

How to order products:
  1. Go to the “Products” page.
  2. Touch the product you want to order.
  3. Choose the amount
  4. Press add to cart
  5. Go to the “Cart” page and click on the product
  6. Press confirm order.
  7. Contact the seller for pickup/delivery
  8. Enjoy!

Founded by students at Brigham Young University - Hawaii, RiceUp Farmers Inc was born out of a strong passion to improve the livelihood of Filipino farmers and to help regenerate agriculture in the Philippines nation. A student-led organization, it seeks to empower the individual farmer by giving farmers access to education, technology, and micro-loans.
